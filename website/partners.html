Why build with Safe Walk

Why build with Safe Walk instead of building it yourself

01
The data integration is already done
We've built the ingestion pipeline for Toronto Police incident data, City of Toronto streetlight data, and community reports. Normalizing municipal open data across different formats, schemas, and update frequencies is unglamorous plumbing work. We've done it so you don't have to.
02
The scoring engine is calibrated
Our safety scoring weights incident proximity, recency, severity, streetlight density, and time of day. It's been tested against known high-incident areas in Toronto and tuned to produce scores that match real-world risk patterns.
03
The community layer creates a moat
Every user who reports an incident makes the safety graph more accurate. More accuracy means more trust, which means more reports. This feedback loop is the competitive advantage: you can't replicate a community data asset from scratch.
04
One API call, no infrastructure
You don't need to host databases, build scoring engines, or manage data pipelines. Send us an origin and destination. We send back routes ranked by safety. Integrate in an afternoon.
